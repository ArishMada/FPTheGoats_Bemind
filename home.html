About Bemind

Our goal

Bemind was made to help teenagers and young adults with stress and other mental health problems. It is designed to make them exercise, meditate and meet people, 3 activities that are said to be the most effective to cure mental problems and get them back into social life. Depression, anxiety and overwhelming stress has been a raising problem among people from 14 to 25 years old, our team came up with an easy yet effective solution to actually try ot help them in the best way possible.

The features

Escapism

In this section of our page, we will be trying to get you to relax and take a step back from your problems by doing some breathing exercises. Alongside those exercises, we aim to improve the quality of your sleep by advising you on your sleep schedule and hopefully get you to be more energetic.

Physical Well-being

Exercizing is well known for its benefits for your physical health, but it also has a lot of psychological benefits such as imrpoving your mood, help fight depression and reduce stress up to 70%. We will give a series fo exercise that are not hard to perform and will be good for both your mental and physical health in the long run. "A healthy body is a healthy mind"

Meet

This page will require you to login with your account because it will match you with other people in the same situation to allow you to interact and try to help each others get back on your feet. socializing does not only staves off feelings of loliness, it will reduce your stress to talk to someone about your problems. We want to give the opportunity to use technology to bring socialization to people not make them seek it themselves by creating random groups
