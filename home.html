Escapism

Are you stressed? Overwhelmed? Sad or just unhappy?
It's totally normal, and most importantly, it's fixable. Let's take a deep breath and forget this stressful world for a few minutes and you will feel better. Bemind offers relaxation techniques that will help you go through a stressful day/week/month and get you the calm you need to live at a 100%. It will make you feel happier, and more relaxed in order to make you enjoy life.

Physical Well-being

You have always thought of getting in shape, always of starting to do some exercise or you are already practicing regularly. In any case, sport is said to be the best activity to improve your mood and that is our goal too. We have a list of exercises, a schedule to follow that will not make you too tired but will help you reconnect with you body, we are here to help you get to your body goal. Beginner or advanced level, all are available for you to go at your own pace

Meet

Meeting new cna be good for your mental health, it can get you out of that bubble of loneliness you create around yourself when you start feeling down. Although, it's hard to get back into the real world and start socializing again after cutting everyone out. But don't worry, Bemind is here to help you meet new people that had or are having the same experience as you, you could join a group to talk about your problems. And that's not all of it; events will also be organized to help the poor, better the environment or just have fun. Hope you'll enjoy.

About Bemind

Our goal

Bemind was made to help teenagers and young adults with stress and other mental health problems. It is designed to make them exercise, meditate and meet people, 3 activities that are said to be the most effective to cure mental problems and get them back into social life. Depression, anxiety and overwhelming stress has been a raising problem among people from 14 to 25 years old, our team came up with an easy yet effective solution to actually try ot help them in the best way possible.
